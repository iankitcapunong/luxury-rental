About Us

A house, born from a gap in the market.

Several years ago, two great minds came together and began a journey in search of luxury transport. To their astonishment, there was a gap in the market crying for luxury travel. From there, Luxury Transport was born, and in turn, Luxury Transport is striving to become one of the leaders in bespoke luxury travel, changing travel into an experience and, in time, a way of life.

We are committed to providing luxury travel that is also environmentally friendly. As we intend to be here for many years to come, here at Luxury Transport it is imperative that we have an electric option on the horizon.
